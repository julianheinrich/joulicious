## Storage
- Store dry pasta in airtight container in cool, dry place
- Dry pasta lasts 1-2 years
- Cooked pasta: refrigerate in airtight container
- Use cooked pasta within 3-5 days
- Can freeze cooked pasta for up to 2 months
