## Storage
- Store uncooked rice in airtight container in cool, dry place
- White rice lasts 4-5 years when properly stored
- Cooked rice: refrigerate within 2 hours
- Use cooked rice within 3-4 days
- Can freeze cooked rice for up to 6 months
