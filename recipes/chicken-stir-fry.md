# Chicken Stir-Fry with Rice

A quick and healthy dinner that combines tender chicken with vegetables over fluffy rice.

## Information

- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 20 minutes
- **Total Time:** 35 minutes
- **Servings:** 4
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Tags:** dinner, healthy, quick, kid-friendly, asian

## Ingredients

- 1 lb [chicken breast](../ingredients/chicken-breast.md), cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 cups [white rice](../ingredients/rice-white.md), cooked
- 1 [onion](../ingredients/onion.md), sliced
- 1 [tomato](../ingredients/tomato.md), diced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- [Salt](../ingredients/salt.md) and [pepper](../ingredients/pepper.md) to taste
- Optional: garlic, ginger, vegetables

## Equipment

- Large skillet or wok
- Rice cooker or pot
- Cutting board
- Knife

## Instructions

1. **Cook the rice:** Prepare rice according to package directions. Set aside.

2. **Prep ingredients:** Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Slice onion and dice tomato.

3. **Heat the pan:** Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

4. **Cook the chicken:** Add chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes until golden and cooked through. Season with salt and pepper.

5. **Add vegetables:** Add onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add tomato and cook for 2 minutes.

6. **Season:** Add soy sauce and stir to combine. Cook for 1-2 minutes.

7. **Serve:** Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.

## Tips

- **Prep ahead:** Cut vegetables and chicken the night before
- **High heat:** Use high heat for authentic stir-fry texture
- **Don't overcrowd:** Cook in batches if needed for best browning
- **Customize:** Add your favorite vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, or snap peas

## Storage

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in microwave or on stovetop.

## Variations

- **Spicy version:** Add chili flakes or sriracha
- **Sweet and sour:** Add pineapple chunks and sweet and sour sauce
- **Teriyaki style:** Use teriyaki sauce instead of soy sauce
- **Vegetarian:** Replace chicken with tofu or extra vegetables
